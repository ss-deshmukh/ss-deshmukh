# AI Essay Evaluator for Students

March 2024 - April 2024&#x20;

Synopsis:

* The AI Essay Evaluator is a web platform I developed to provide an affordable alternative to the official GRE essay evaluation service.
* Users can submit their essays for comprehensive analysis, receiving instant feedback on structure, coherence, grammar, and argument strength. This tool helps users improve their writing skills efficiently and practice comprehensively without worrying about costs.
