# Healthcare Management Platform

***

July 2023 - Oct 2024

Synopsis :&#x20;

* Secure Patient Management: Access verified patient records through Health ID integration, with blockchain-backed security ensuring data integrity and privacy
* Smart Appointment Management: Streamlined scheduling with real-time availability updates and automated confirmation system
* Integrated Medical Records: Easy access to comprehensive patient histories with consent-based sharing, allowing doctors to make informed decisions quickly
* Regulatory Compliance: Built-in adherence to healthcare data regulations, including HIPAA and GDPR standards
* Future-Ready Architecture: Hybrid storage solution combining blockchain's security with traditional databases' efficiency, making it scalable for growing practices
