# Palkhi Yatra

***

## Why, When, How?

* I started the Palkhi Yatra endeavour to continue my father's standalone effort to provide food to the pilgrims participating in the Pandharpur Waari (pilgrim). From the very beginning, our primary goal has been to offer food to pilgrims during the challenging stretches of their journey.
* The Pandharpur Waari is not just a pilgrimage for me; it is a festival I have watched and cherished since childhood. Witnessing the gnawing problems like lack of organization, food waste, and the spread of illness due to food poisoning among pilgrims left me feeling helpless in my younger years.
* In 2018, I resolved to solve these problems head-on and make a positive difference by bringing people together who shared the same goal and created Palkhi Yatra to properly manage the food supply and ensure a healthy journey for the pilgrims.
