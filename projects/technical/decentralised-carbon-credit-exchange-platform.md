# Decentralised Carbon Credit Exchange Platform

Jan 23 - March 23

Synopsis:

* Developed by a global team from different countries as part of my Master's Thesis, this project guides the ICT sector to achieve Net Zero Emission Targets.
* Utilizing the Plutus Language on the Cardano Blockchain, the Decentralized Application leverages the Science Based Target (SBTi) Methodology to help ICT companies halve emissions by 2030 and reach net zero by 2050.

<summary>Tech Stack</summary>
